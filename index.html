SPEAKERS

Jerome Saddi
VESSEL TRAFFIC MANAGEMENT SYSTEM

An engineer guides us on how to track incoming and outgoing ships, determine their distance, and verify if they are within designated boundaries. Monitoring the location and status of ships is crucial for ensuring their safety and security, as well as predicting arrival times. This information is valuable for developing future predictive patterns.

Gretchel Listana
HYTEC POWER INC.

During our tour with Gretchel from Hytec Power Inc., I felt at home exploring their advanced facilities and innovative power solutions. Gretchel's expertise and passion for sustainable energy were clear as she guided us through their impressive generators and renewable technologies. Her insightful & friendly approach made the tour enjoyable and inspiring.

Adrian Francisco Salda
LIGHT RAIL TRANSIT AUTHORITY

Sir Adrian teaches us about train operations, safety precautions, and the do's and don'ts for passenger safety. He showed us their software for tracking train status and explained the technical details of train movement and the rails that serve as their road, providing a comprehensive understanding of train operations.

Karla Legaspi
QBO INNOVATION HUB

She explained to us what is their company all about like what does it do, who are the people they need in their company, and explained what is start ups which is about they say that that companies or people who are just starting and that they need people like that most especially students who just graduated it will really be a start up and they will be happy to accept fresh starting graduates there."

Rose Anne Caraan
PACKETWORX INC.

She explained their company's mission, collaborations, and purpose, focusing on creating IoT devices that connect multiple devices. She showcased their advanced IoT products and explained the hardware functions and data storage. Data collected by the hardware is sent to their online dashboard for future research, highlighting the technology's practical benefits.

Dave Del Rosario
FIRSTAVIATION ACADEMY

An FAA staff member guided us through the software used for teaching students how to fly an airplane virtually. He explained the software's features, which simulate actual flight scenarios, allowing students to experience flying without being in a real airplane. This detailed discussion highlighted the software's effectiveness in aviation training.

Brian Tanseng
98 LABS INC.

Brian Tan Seng serves as the CEO of 98Labs, specializing in the development of websites and software for diverse companies. Their focus lies in crafting unique software products, setting them apart in the industry.

Berna Puyat
NATIONAL MUSEUM OF THEPHILIPPINES

She teaches Philippine history with engaging stories and insights, catering to our curiosity by uncovering Filipino cultural and historical treasures. Berna's clear explanations and lively anecdotes bring history to life.

Arnel Gonzales
PAGASA WEATHER STATION

He explained to us how they determine upcoming weather conditions for days, weeks, and months ahead through their software and hardware. They also analyze wind patterns to enhance their weather forecasting capabilities, which aids in predicting weather patterns.
